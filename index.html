Now

Some of us have online empires to build and can ill afford to engage in frivolous small talk. So when someone we know asks us "what's up," we simply send them a link to our now pages.

These pages often feature what we're currently watching, reading, or listening to.

(On a serious note: if you have a personal website, you should make one too.) It's an underrated way to reflect on your priorities.
